    What We Do

    Let’s Stay Home is a cocktail delivery service run by professional bartenders. We’ve worked in bars across London, Asia and New Zealand and it’s our passion to bring exceptional, handmade, fresh drinks to you. Established in May 2020, Let’s Stay Home brings bar quality cocktails to your doorstep. We take care of all the measuring, shaking and juicing to deliver ready to drink cocktails to you. We regularly update our menus to utilize the freshest ingredients we can get our hands on and are always up for the challenge of designing a bespoke drink for you. You can find our cocktails under the fresh cocktail tab or get in touch to find out more.

    Fresh Cocktails

    Delivered to your door seven days a week with same day delivery available on all orders placed before 3pm. Simply email, txt or call to place your order. Our delivery radius covers 8 miles of SM, Surrey. Please note all our cocktails are made fresh just before delivery and are best enjoyed within 48 hours. (Photos of house cocktails)
